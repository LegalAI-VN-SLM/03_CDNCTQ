\section{Project Timeline and Implementation Plan}

\subsection{Work Package Breakdown and Implementation Strategy}
The execution of the proposed research is organized into three unified Work Packages (WPs) to ensure a systematic progression from data collection to pipeline development, validation, and documentation:
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{WP1: Selective RAG Memory System:} Coordinates the crawl and structured parsing of Vietnamese legal documents---scoped to the two testbed sectors (labour law in full as the primary corpus, and a curated tax-law slice as the secondary) so that ingestion remains feasible within six months---indexing them with dense bi-encoder models, constructing citation graphs, and implementing the dynamic legal importance and Ebbinghaus-based consolidation and decay rerankers.
    \item \textbf{WP2: Controllable Legal Unlearning Gate:} Implements the $P+Q$ optimization policies for forget requests, output compliance filtering, and the immutable logging audits to ensure regulatory safety.
    \item \textbf{WP3: System Benchmarking and Validation:} Sets up static RAG baselines, executes ablation studies on the importance features, measures unlearning effectiveness and robustness, and evaluates accuracy on Vietnamese datasets like VLQA \parencite{nguyen2025vlqacomprehensivelargehighquality}, ViLegalNLI \parencite{duong2026vilegalnlinaturallanguageinference}, and LegalSLM \parencite{le-etal-2025-overview}.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Risk Assessment and Mitigation Strategies}
To ensure the smooth execution of the project, potential technical and operational risks are identified along with corresponding mitigation strategies:

\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
    \item \textbf{Risk 1: Metadata Inaccuracy}
    \begin{itemize}
        \item \textit{Impact \& Probability:} High Impact / Moderate Probability
        \item \textit{Mitigation Strategy:} Implement a double-pass manual verification check on a randomized sample of 5\% of the ingested legal documents to validate validity dates and citation links.
    \end{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Risk 2: Retrieval Recall Failure}
    \begin{itemize}
        \item \textit{Impact \& Probability:} High Impact / Low Probability
        \item \textit{Mitigation Strategy:} Integrate a hybrid search mechanism (combining dense bi-encoder embeddings with sparse BM25 keyword matching) to act as a robust fallback.
    \end{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Risk 3: Computational Bottleneck}
    \begin{itemize}
        \item \textit{Impact \& Probability:} Moderate Impact / Moderate Probability
        \item \textit{Mitigation Strategy:} Pre-compute dense embeddings offline and restrict the hyperparameter tuning search grids via Bayesian optimization.
    \end{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Risk 4: Access Restriction Leakage}
    \begin{itemize}
        \item \textit{Impact \& Probability:} High Impact / Low Probability
        \item \textit{Mitigation Strategy:} Conduct automated validation audits on the access-control filter policies before deploying vector index updates.
    \end{itemize}
\end{itemize}
