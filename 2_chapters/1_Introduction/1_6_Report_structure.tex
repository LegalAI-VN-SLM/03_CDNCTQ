\section{Report Structure}

This research proposal is organized into four active chapters:
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Chapter 1 (Introduction):} Outlines the background and necessity of the research, states the general and specific research objectives, formulates the research questions and hypotheses, defines the operational scope, and summarizes the study's core contributions.
    \item \textbf{Chapter 2 (Background and Literature Review):} Provides a systematic survey of continual learning paradigms in language models, outlines the limitations of parameter-efficient tuning, compares RAG memory and unlearning frameworks, and surveys existing Vietnamese legal datasets.
    \item \textbf{Chapter 3 (Proposed Methods and Models):} Details the overall system architecture, defines the non-parametric legal importance score and memory stratification tiers, formalizes the selective retrieval algorithm, and outlines the $P+Q$ machine unlearning gate implementation.
    \item \textbf{Chapter 4 (Expected Outcomes and Research Plan):} Maps the research objectives to tangible scientific deliverables and quantitative acceptance thresholds, establishes academic publication plans, details the Work Package dependencies, and outlines the project budget and implementation timeline.
\end{itemize}

Experimental results and final conclusions are deferred to the full dissertation and are excluded from this proposal.
