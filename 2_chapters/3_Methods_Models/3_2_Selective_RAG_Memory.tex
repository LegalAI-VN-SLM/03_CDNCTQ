\section{Expected Results}

The primary objective of this research is to develop a robust, compliant, and computationally efficient non-parametric continual learning framework for Vietnamese legal language modeling. The expected scientific and technology deliverables, mapped directly to the project's specific objectives and their quantitative acceptance thresholds, are detailed in Table~\ref{tab:scientific_products}.

\begin{table}[p]
\scriptsize
\setlength{\tabcolsep}{3pt}
\renewcommand{\arraystretch}{0.9}
\setlist[itemize]{nosep,leftmargin=*}
\centering
\caption{Mapping of Specific Objectives to Deliverables and Acceptance Thresholds.}
\label{tab:scientific_products}
\begin{tabularx}{\textwidth}{lXX}
\hline
\textbf{Specific Objective} & \textbf{Expected Deliverable} & \textbf{Quantitative Acceptance Threshold} \\
\hline
\textbf{Objective 1:} Selective Legal Memory RAG & A cognitive-based RAG memory indexing and reranking software module. & 
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
    \item Active vector index size reduction of $\ge 30\%$ compared to static RAG.
    \item Retrieval accuracy (Recall@10) maintained within $\pm 2\%$ of the full-index baseline.
\end{itemize} \\
\hline
\textbf{Objective 2:} Controllable RAG Unlearning & A behavioral unlearning gate module implementing $P+Q$ optimization and compliance filters. & 
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
    \item Effectiveness: ROUGE-L score $\le 0.15$ on target unlearned queries.
    \item Harmlessness: Performance on unrelated active queries maintained at $\ge 95\%$ of baseline.
    \item Robustness: True Positive Rate (TPR) $\le 1\%$ at a 1\% False Positive Rate (FPR) limit under adversarial jailbreak queries.
\end{itemize} \\
\hline
\textbf{Objective 3:} Evaluation & A standardized benchmark evaluation suite for Vietnamese legal continual learning. & 
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
    \item Successful deployment on VLQA \parencite{nguyen2025vlqacomprehensivelargehighquality}, ViLegalNLI \parencite{duong2026vilegalnlinaturallanguageinference}, and LegalSLM \parencite{le-etal-2025-overview} datasets.
\end{itemize} \\
\hline
\end{tabularx}
\end{table}

\subsection{Academic and Training Outcomes}
In addition to the software artifacts, this project aims to contribute to academic training and scientific literature:
\begin{enumerate}
    \item \textbf{Scientific Publications:} 
    \begin{itemize}
        \item One (1) paper submitted to a reputable international conference in NLP/AI (e.g., PACLIC, KSE, or the VLSP workshop).
        \item One (1) journal paper submitted to an ISI/Scopus-indexed journal (such as IEEE Access or similar computer science publications).
    \end{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Human Resource Training:}
    \begin{itemize}
        \item Support the completion of one (1) Master's thesis in Computer Science or Artificial Intelligence.
        \item Support the graduation projects of two (2) undergraduate students in Software Engineering or Data Science.
    \end{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Open-Source Assets:}
    \begin{itemize}
        \item Release of a public GitHub repository containing the complete implementation code, evaluation scripts, and metadata-augmented indexes.
        \item Publication of the evaluation metrics and baseline results to foster reproducibility in Vietnamese legal-CL studies.
    \end{itemize}
\end{enumerate}
